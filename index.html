THE LEGEND

About Prabhu Deva

⭐ India's
Michael Jackson

Known as the "Indian Michael Jackson," Prabhu Deva is one of India's most iconic dancers, choreographers, and film directors.

With a career spanning over three decades, Prabhu Deva has revolutionized Indian dance with his unique fusion of classical, contemporary, and street dance styles. His electrifying performances have captivated audiences worldwide, and his choreography has defined some of Bollywood's most memorable dance sequences.

Now, for the first time, he brings his legendary talent to Raleigh, North Carolina as part of his exclusive North America Tour 2026. This is your once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to witness the master in action!
